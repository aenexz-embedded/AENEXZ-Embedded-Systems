Problem Statement
Write bare metal C code to alternately blink two LEDs connected to pins PA1 and PB14 of BluePill, with a toggle interval of 500ms.
Requirements:
- When PA1's LED is ON, PB14's LED must be OFF, and vice versa, the two must never be on (or off) at the same time.
- Use direct register writes only.
- Clock-enable bits and GPIO addresses for both ports must be located in the Reference Manual, not copy-pasted.
